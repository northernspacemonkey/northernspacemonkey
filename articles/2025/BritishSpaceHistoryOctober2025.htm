British Space Industry History

October 20th 2025

British space programs have been operating for 70 years, despite the lack of media highlighting. In a beautifully presented history section of their website, Space South Central have provided a history of the innovation and technical progress Britain has made since 1955.

Walking through seventy years of progress, it's striking how many world-first moments the British engineering teams have achieved.

From early days with rocketry, through the development of highly adaptable satellite technology, it's good to see the forward-thinking progress driving innovation around the world.
