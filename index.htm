Adventures in Tech and Space

The Northern Space Monkey is a systems engineer working in the space industry. On a daily basis NSM is communicating with satellite equipment and ground stations across the planet. In the course of their work they find interesting things to talk about, investigate and create solutions for. This site is the combination of scraps of paper notes, conversations with peers and development work so that I can keep a record of fun, interesting findings and useful support to help others in their adventures.

If you are interested in all things space, talking to spacecraft and doing fun things with data feeds from orbital craft, this is the place for you; Welcome, fellow geek! Grab a cup of coffee and join me on this journey of discovery, tinkering and laughter.

Discover more in the Projects section, dive into the Code section or follow along in the Information section.
