Adventures in Space Tech

The Northern Space Monkey is a systems engineer working in the space industry. On a daily basis, NSM is communicating with Low Earth Orbit satellite equipment and ground stations across the planet. In the course of work, NSM finds interesting things to talk about, investigate and create solutions for.

This site is the combination of notes written on scraps of paper, conversations with peers and development work undertken. Shared here, so that NSM can keep a record of fun and interesting findings, providing useful support to help himself and others in their adventures.

If you are interested in all things space, talking to spacecraft and doing fun things with data feeds from orbital platforms, then this is the place for you!
Welcome, fellow space and tech geek, to The Northern Space Monkey's playground. Grab a cup of coffee and a notepad and join me on this journey of discovery, tinkering and space adventure.
